Create a Safe and Comfortable Space: Set up a cozy area with a bed, scratching post, and toys where your cat can feel secure. A comfortable environment helps your cat adjust and feel at home.
Build a Routine: Cats thrive on routine. Establish regular feeding times, play sessions, and grooming habits. This consistency helps your cat feel secure and strengthens your bond.
Show Love and Patience: Spend quality time with your cat, offering affection and understanding. Every cat has its own personality, so be patient and let your bond grow naturally over time.
